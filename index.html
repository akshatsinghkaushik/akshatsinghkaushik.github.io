About Me


I’m Akshat Kaushik, a final year Bachelor of Software Engineering Student from the University of Technology Sydney. I know titles don’t mean a great deal these days, so I’ll lay it out for you. Software Engineering isn’t a degree I ever dreamed of pursuing but a qualification I chose to pursue to compliment my passion for Software Development. I was fortunate enough to go to a school where Computers was part of our daily curriculum as early as 3rd Grade, ever since I’ve only ever been introduced to more and more things Computers till Middle School (6th Grade) when we were introduced to programming with computers for the first time. Its not until much after that I realised how good I was at it as it kept getting progressively more complex for the class but not me. Raised in a family of doctors, it was difficult to convince a stream so different from the family default, but I managed to find my way like I always do.

In my three years studying Software Engineering at UTS, I’ve significantly honed my skills to code, the one skill I enjoy practicing for days on end. I’m an active member of ProgSoc (Progamming Society) and CyberSecurity Society at UTS and will often find me participating in Hackathons and other community events hosted by them. I also work part time, after hours for a UTS:Startups startup called CryptoSpend where I’m the only experienced Java developer helping them develop the backend behind their crypto based finance platform.

Please feel welcome to explore all the public projects I’ve worked and am working on, on Github.
